> **Redirect**: Bottom-fermented beers are lagers — beers fermented with *Saccharomyces pastorianus* at cold temperatures (4–12°C), where yeast sinks to the bottom. See [[fermentation-types]] for the biochemistry and [[beer-types]] for the full style taxonomy of lagers (Pilsner, Märzen, Bock, Dark Lager).
